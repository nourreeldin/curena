# Data Deletion Instructions

If you want to delete your account or any personal data related to the **Medication Management App**, follow the instructions below:

### 1. Send a Data Deletion Request
Email us at:

**noureeldinhossamm@gmail.com**

Include the following:
- Subject: *Data Deletion Request*
- Your account email address
- Optional: Any additional data you want removed

### 2. What Happens Next
Once we receive your request:
- Your account will be permanently deleted from our Supabase database.
- All personal data, medication logs, and associated health data will be removed.
- Deletion will be completed within **30 days**.
- You will receive a confirmation email once the process is finished.
